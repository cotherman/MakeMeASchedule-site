Product Description

Make Me A Schedule is a workforce management platform that facilitates real-time shift coordination. We help employers and employees stay in sync through automated notifications, shift assignments, and time-off request processing.

Employee SMS Setup

To begin receiving your shift assignments and schedule updates via SMS, please follow the instructions below:

Text START to (972) 632-5084

By texting START, you affirmatively opt-in to receive transactional scheduling alerts from Make Me A Schedule. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel at any time, HELP for help.

How Enrollment Works

  1. Employees are briefed on the scheduling system during their workplace onboarding.
  2. To initiate the service, the employee sends a text message with the keyword START to the system number.
  3. The system verifies the mobile number against the employee's record and sends a confirmation.
  4. No messages are sent until this user-initiated text is received.
